### 🦟 The Mosquito
*Fast, annoying, and thirsty for blood!*

The Mosquito is a high-risk, hyper-mobile vampire that thrives in the center of the swarm. It trades raw durability and damage for insane attack speed and self-healing. You start incredibly frail, but the more blood you siphon from the aliens, the faster and deadlier you become!

**The Stats:**
🟢 **+20%** Lifesteal 
🟢 **+1%** Attack Speed for every **1%** Lifesteal you have 
🟢 **+15%** Dodge 
🟢 **+15%** Speed 
🟢 Lifesteal modifications are increased by **50%**
🔴 **-10** Max HP 
🔴 **-100** HP Regeneration 
🔴 **-30%** Damage 
🔴 **-50** Range 

**Playstyle Tips:**
* **Blood Frenzy:** Your Attack Speed scales directly off your Lifesteal. Because Lifesteal gains are amplified by 50%, every Medical item or Lifesteal upgrade you find will cause your attack speed to skyrocket!
* **Up Close and Personal:** With a massive penalty to Range and Damage, you have to get dangerously close to the swarm to deal damage.
* **No Natural Healing:** Your HP Regeneration is permanently crippled. If you get hit, your *only* way to recover is to keep attacking and rely on your Lifesteal to instantly top you back up! Keep moving, keep dodging, and keep biting!
